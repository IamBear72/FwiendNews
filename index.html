We focus on real reporting, honest updates, and straightforward coverage instead of dressing up noise as news.

Helping Fwiends avoid fake news

Our goal is to give Fwiends a place they can trust when they want the day's biggest updates without fake news getting in the way. However, we may not be able to give fact 100% of the time and sometimes something may slip through, but we will try our best to keep the news as up to date as possible.
